Adeola Johnson is a visionary leader in the African digital banking space with over 20 years of experience. As the Vice Chairman of the Board of Trustees, he plays a pivotal role in steering the strategic direction of the CIO & C-Suite Club Africa.

Previously, he has led digital transformation initiatives across multiple financial institutions, focusing on cloud infrastructure, cybersecurity, and financial inclusion through technology.

He is a frequent speaker at global technology forums and an advocate for young African tech talent, mentoring over 500 professionals across the continent.
