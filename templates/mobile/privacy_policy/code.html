Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you join the CIO & C-Suite Club Africa, including your name, professional title, company, email address, and LinkedIn profile.

Additionally, we may collect technical data such as your IP address, device type, and app usage patterns to improve our executive networking experience.

How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used to:

  • Facilitate high-level networking opportunities among members.
  • Send exclusive invitations to club events and summits.
  • Personalize the content and insights delivered via the platform.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our professional community.
