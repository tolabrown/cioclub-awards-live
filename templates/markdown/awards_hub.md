## About the Event
The CIO & C-Suite Conference & Awards Africa stands as the continent’s premier platform, celebrating visionary leadership and technological excellence. Now in its sixth year, this pan-African event showcases innovation, transformation, and enterprise leadership across Africa.

Beyond individual excellence, the Awards spotlight forward-thinking organizations that have embedded innovation at their core. These enterprises are redefining business models, nurturing digital-first cultures, and creating measurable value and impact across industries.

Complementing the Awards is the CIO & C-Suite Conference, a premier gathering of Africa’s top business and technology leaders. The Conference serves as a dynamic platform for dialogue, learning, and collaboration. Through keynote sessions, panel discussions, breakout workshops, and exhibitions, participants explore the future of technology, leadership, and enterprise transformation.

Spanning 15 countries including Kenya, Uganda, Togo, Rwanda, Nigeria, Ghana, Tunisia, Egypt, Angola, Morocco, Senegal, South Africa, Zambia, Cameroon, and Côte d’Ivoire, the Awards and Conference celebrate the diversity of leadership and innovation shaping Africa’s digital future.

## Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to common questions about the CIO & C-Suite Conference & Awards Africa.

### Who Can Attend?
The CIO & C-Suite Conference & Awards Africa is exclusively designed for senior technology and business leaders, including CIOs, CTOs, CEOs, and other C-Suite executives. It’s a valuable event for IT directors, technology innovators, and digital transformation specialists.

### How do I register to attend?
You can register directly through this [Link](https://rsvp.app-wt.com.ng/cio.c-suite-award-2025) or on our website by completing the registration form and securing your ticket. Payment is processed online for a seamless experience.

### Are the Awards Open for Nominations?
Nominations for this year have closed as of August. Submissions will reopen next year, and updated timelines will be shared on our website and communication channels.

### Where will the 2025 edition take place?
The 2025 Conference and Awards will be hosted in The Civic Center, Lagos, Nigeria bringing together leaders from across the continent.

### How can my company become a sponsor or exhibitor?
Partnership opportunities are available across Platinum, Gold, Silver, Media, and Exhibitor categories. Interested organizations can reach out to Racheal at [racheal@edniesalconsulting.com](mailto:racheal@edniesalconsulting.com).

### Will there be opportunities for networking?
Yes. The Conference is designed to maximize networking opportunities through dedicated sessions, exhibitions, and social events where leaders can connect and collaborate.

### Will the event be streamed online?
Yes. A virtual attendance option will be available for participants unable to join physically. Details would be communicated in due time.
